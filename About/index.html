Flexible actions for all ...

The vsSolutionBuildEvent (vsSBE) - are all related projects to support the product line of MS Visual Studio & MSBuild tools.

It presents advanced handler of most events.

Full control and flexible multi actions for basic events and others additional, such as:

  • CommandEvent, Errors, Warnings, Cancel-Build, Output-Build, Transmitter, Logging

Ability to handle events for all subprojects at once in solution as Solution-Events or individually for each.

Available different modes for all of what you want:

  • Files Mode, Operation Mode, Interpreter Mode, Script Mode, Targets Mode, C# Mode

Also supports additional MSBuild & SBE-Scripts engine for advanced usage. Supports the CI /Build Servers, Command-Line mode and many other features for convenience of your work with the build, tests, debugging, versioning, CI, logging, etc.,

Works on the Event-Actions model, and ready for anything for what you need.
